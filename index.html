What is card counting?

Card counting is a strategy used to track the ratio of high to low cards remaining in the deck, giving players an edge over the house.

Is card counting illegal?

Nope! Card counting is perfectly legal, but casinos might ask you to leave if they catch you. Think of it as a game of cat and mouse!

How much can I make counting cards?

How much you can earn as a card counter is dependent on game selection, strategy, and your bankroll/bet spread. Our most advanced card counters make individually over $500 an hour in EV and can make more as part of a team.

Do I need special tools?

Yes! Our suite of tools is designed to enhance your counting skills, making you a formidable opponent at the tables. Card counters must be able to simulate their earnings, drill and track their accuracy/speed, and have a tool to help discover beatable blackjack games. All of which we provide!

Can anyone become a card counter?

Absolutely! With our training, anyone can master the art of card counting. Just bring your brain and come join us!
